## Project Overview

Retail businesses generate vast amounts of customer data every day. Understanding customer purchasing behavior is essential for improving marketing strategies, customer retention, and overall business growth.

This project analyzes retail customer data to identify meaningful customer segments based on demographic characteristics and purchasing behavior. Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), feature engineering, and customer analytics, the project converts raw retail data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement

Businesses often treat every customer similarly despite significant differences in purchasing behavior. This leads to ineffective marketing campaigns, poor customer retention, and missed revenue opportunities.

This project demonstrates how customer segmentation can help businesses identify valuable customer groups and create personalized engagement strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics.
- Study purchasing behavior.
